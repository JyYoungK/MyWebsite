Project

I love playing poker, especially with my good-old friends. At the same time, I also like to change the rules of the original game to my own. One day, I decided to create my own version of poker. I named it AZ poker because there is a wild card called "Z" that can turn into any card that will always change your hand to a better hand as possible. The highest hand you can make is what I call "Emperor Straight Flush, meaning your hand consists of ""A","K","Q","J" with same suit and "Z". The deck consists of 66 cards because it has 5 suits and the wild card. I used my math skill I acquired in school to calculate the possibility of the highest winning hand and it turns out, you have 0.0023% chance to get it!You can try playing it by downloading directly from my github. I used language python to build it few years ago but I am currently working on re-modeling the game using node.js to play it on discord.

This game is my first game I made using a programming language. I first used python to make it few years ago and I re-modeled it using node.js to play it on discord. I find games that requires strategic thinking not only challenging but fun as well. The rule is simple. You are standing on a random position of clock. On your turn, you have a choice to move either up or down by 1 or 2. There are 3 obstacles to avoid, which are the second-hand, the minute-hand, and the hour-hand. All 3 moves differently but in a consistant pattern. Your challenge is to avoid them for 10 turns. The tip is to figure out your current position, where the hands are located and how many steps it moves. This game may seem entirely luck based but it can be done using a strategy. If you like strategy games, you should try this game out by inviting my bot to your discord channel. I challenge you to beat the hardest mode. Click the icon, invite the bot to your channel, type !clock to begin.
